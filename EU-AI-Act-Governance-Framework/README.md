Under the EU AI Act, systems are governed based on the severity of their impact. This framework provides the controls necessary for each tier:

### üî¥ Unacceptable Risk (Prohibited)
* **Scope:** Systems that manipulate human behavior, perform indiscriminate social scoring, or utilize public biometric identification.
* **Counsel Action:** Full decommissioning of systems or pivot to compliant alternatives.

### üü° High-Risk (Regulated)
* **Scope:** AI in critical infrastructure, medical device diagnostics, HR recruitment, and credit scoring.
* **Governance Requirement:** Mandatory **Fundamental Rights Impact Assessments (FRIA)** and continuous post-market monitoring.

### üîµ Limited Risk (Transparency)
* **Scope:** Chatbots, deepfakes, and generative content.
* **Governance Requirement:** Explicit disclosure to users that they are interacting with an automated agent.
